Question: What is the Laplace transform?
Answer: Integral transforms in applied mathematics

Question: What is the Laplace transform used for?
Answer: Transformation of linear differential equations and convolutional integrals into algebraic ones, making them easier to solve

Question: What are the main properties of the Laplace transform?
Answer: The Laplace transform has many properties that are used to solve problems, including the following:

1. Linearity
2. Shift Theorem
3. Integration theorem
4. Dilations
5. Convolution

Question: What is the equation for the Laplace transform?
Answer: Laplace transform is given by the formula $\mathscr{L}\{f(t)\} = F(s) = \int_0^\infty e^{-st}f(t)dt$
