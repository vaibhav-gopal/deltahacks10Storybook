Question: What is the Laplace transform?
Answer: Laplace transform is an integral transform used in applied mathematics.

Question: What are integral transforms? 
Answer: Integral transforms are a subset of applied mathematics that involves transforming a function by integrating it with another function. 

Question: What is the purpose of Laplace transforming a function? 
Answer: Transforming a function via the Laplace method can make integrating or solving differential equations easier, especially when the function is not a simple one.
